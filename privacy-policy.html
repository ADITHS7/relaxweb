RelaxApp operates the RelaxApp mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application. By using RelaxApp, you agree to the practices described below.

01

About RelaxApp

RelaxApp is a peer-to-peer calling platform designed to help users find emotional comfort, reduce stress, and talk with trained listeners.

RelaxApp does not provide medical advice, psychological therapy, counseling, or healthcare services. Conversations are intended for general well-being and supportive listening purposes only.

02

Information We Collect

a) Account Information

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Profile details

b) Usage Information

  • App interaction data
  • Call session information
  • Device information
  • Log data and diagnostics

c) Payment Information

Payments are processed through secure third-party payment providers. RelaxApp does not store card or banking details on its servers.

03

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Enable calling services
  • Improve user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Maintain platform safety
  • Prevent fraud or misuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

04

Call Privacy

RelaxApp respects user confidentiality.

  • Calls are not publicly shared.
  • Call data may be monitored only for safety, abuse prevention, or service improvement.
  • Users should avoid sharing highly sensitive personal or financial information during calls.

05
