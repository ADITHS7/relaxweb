Privacy Policy for Freelancer App

Last updated: April 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Freelancer app ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. By using Freelancer, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Phone number — used for account creation and OTP-based login
  • Profile information — name, age, languages, qualifications, bio
  • Bank/UPI details — for processing freelancer earnings withdrawals

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information — device model, OS version, unique device ID
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging token — for push notifications
  • Call records — duration, timestamps, call type (voice/video)
  • Earnings and transaction history

2. Permissions We Use

  • Camera — required for video calls between users and freelancers
  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO) — required for voice and video calls
  • Modify Audio Settings — to manage call audio routing (speaker, earpiece, Bluetooth)
  • Bluetooth — to enable Bluetooth headset connectivity during calls
  • Internet & Network State — required for app functionality and call signaling
  • Post Notifications — to notify you of incoming calls and messages
  • Vibrate — to alert you of incoming calls
  • Wake Lock — to keep the screen active during calls
  • Foreground Service (Phone Call, Microphone, Camera) — to maintain active calls in the background
  • Use Full Screen Intent — to display incoming call screens like a native phone call
  • Manage Own Calls / Call Phone — required for the in-app calling system
  • Receive Boot Completed — to ensure the app stays connected for incoming calls after device restart
  • Read Phone State — to manage call interruptions during system phone calls
  • Battery Optimization — to ensure incoming calls reach you reliably
  • Write External Storage — to save call logs and app data

We do NOT record or store the content of voice or video calls. All calls are peer-to-peer and end-to-end through our calling provider (ZegoCloud).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Freelancer service
  • To authenticate users via OTP
  • To process payments and freelancer earnings
  • To enable voice and video calls
  • To send service-related notifications
  • To prevent fraud and abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, push notifications, analytics — Firebase Privacy Policy
  • ZegoCloud — voice and video call infrastructure — ZegoCloud Privacy Policy
  • Razorpay — payment processing — Razorpay Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers and transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS). We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share data only:

  • With service providers (listed above) necessary to operate the app
  • To comply with legal requirements or valid government requests
  • To protect our rights, safety, and the safety of our users

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Account Deletion

You may request account deletion by emailing us. Upon deletion, your personal information, call history, and profile will be permanently removed from our active systems. Some data may be retained as required by law or for fraud prevention.

9. Children's Privacy

Freelancer is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
