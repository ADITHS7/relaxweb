Delete Account – RelaxApp

Users can request deletion of their RelaxApp account and all associated data at any time.

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the RelaxApp mobile application
  2. Navigate to Profile → Settings
  3. Tap Delete Account

OR

Send a deletion request via email:
📧 rrelaxapp@gmail.com

Data Deleted

  • Profile information
  • Phone number
  • Call history
  • User account data

Data Retained

Payment transaction records may be retained for legal and financial compliance purposes for up to 90 days.
