RelaxApp operates the RelaxApp mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application. By using RelaxApp, you agree to the practices described below.

01

About RelaxApp

RelaxApp is a peer-to-peer calling platform designed to help users find emotional comfort, reduce stress, and talk with trained listeners.

RelaxApp does not provide medical advice, psychological therapy, counseling, or healthcare services. Conversations are intended for general well-being and supportive listening purposes only.

02

Information We Collect

We receive and collect several types of data during your registration and use of our calling service:

a) Account Information

  • Your primary profile registration settings (Name or Nickname)
  • Verified phone number associated with the account
  • Email address provided for updates
  • Custom profile description avatar settings

b) Usage Information

  • App user interactions data logs
  • Voice and video call session time logs
  • User mobile device hardware identifiers
  • App log reports & diagnostic records

c) Payment Information

Payments and coin transactions are handled securely via official third-party payment providers. RelaxApp does not store credit card details or banking credentials on its servers.

03

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to run and improve our services, including to:

  • Create, configure, and manage user accounts
  • Establish and route secure private calling services
  • Personalize and improve the mobile application experience
  • Provide customer support desk assistance
  • Maintain platform security and compliance
  • Identify and prevent platform fraud or abuse
  • Comply with tax and legal requirements

04

Call Privacy

RelaxApp respects user confidentiality and handles calls with strict protection.

  • Calling streams are secure and are not publicly shared or broadcasted.
  • Calling sessions may be monitored only for security audits, harassment investigations, or app performance checks.
  • We strongly advise users to avoid sharing passwords, credit card details, or sensitive personal data with hosts during calls.

05

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Information may be shared only with:

  • Infrastructure service providers required to host or run the calling service
  • Verified third-party payment gateway processors
  • Law enforcement or legal authorities under valid legal summons

06

Data Security

We implement administrative and technical security measures to safeguard user databases. However, because no online connection is 100% secure, we cannot guarantee absolute database security.

07

Account Deletion

Users can request permanent erasure of their profile data directly inside the application settings panel.

  • All personal profiles, usernames, and call logs are deleted or anonymized upon request completion.
  • Certain transactional or purchase history data may be retained temporarily to meet legal and financial compliance rules.

08

Children's Privacy

RelaxApp is strictly for users who are at least 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect, record, or request registration data from minors.

09

Third-Party Services

RelaxApp integrates trusted third-party SDKs to run analytics and payment features. These platforms follow their own privacy rules:

  • Google Firebase (DB, Authentication & Crashlytics)
  • Mobile App Analytics Providers
  • Google Play billing and Payment Gateways

10

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy. Continued use of our calling service after updates have been published signifies your agreement to the new policy terms.
